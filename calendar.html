An Event for Every Season

Spring

Meet our Town Founder: Come meet the woman who started it all. Every spring, Robyn Carr returns home to Virgin River to catch up with the town, see old friends, and make new ones.

Learn more about Robyn Carr
Summer

There's no place to celebrate America's independence like Virgin River. Join us for the annual town picnic. Jack and Preacher will be cooking all weekend, so bring your appetite!

Autumn

Jilly Farms is the place to be when the leaves start to turn. Come to the farm for hay rides, pumpkin carving, and hot apple cider. While you're there, be sure to sample Jilly Farms' assortment of homemade chutneys, salsas, and jams.

Winter

Honor our troops with a stop at the biggest Christmas tree around. Adorned with medals honoring all branches of service, it's a holiday tradition that can't be missed. Stop by Jack's for some hot chocolate and enjoy the Christmas magic.
